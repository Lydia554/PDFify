Learn how to use our PDF Generator with your Shopify store. This guide walks you through step-by-step instructions for generating beautiful, branded PDFs for your products, orders, and customers.

Follow these simple steps to generate your PDFs directly from Shopify data or raw HTML content:

  1. Step 1: Export Shopify Data (JSON) or Raw HTML

    You can either export your Shopify data in JSON format (containing product details, orders, customer data, etc.) or provide raw HTML content that you'd like to convert into a PDF.

    If you choose raw HTML, simply create or modify the HTML document with the content you want to include in your PDF. This could be a customized invoice, product sheet, or any other document designed in HTML.

  2. Step 2: Upload Data or HTML to PDF Generator

    Upload your JSON data or raw HTML file to the PDF Generator interface. For JSON, the system will parse the data and automatically generate the PDF based on the selected template. For raw HTML, the system will directly convert the HTML into a styled PDF document.

  3. Step 3: Choose a PDF Template or Customize Layout

    Select a pre-designed PDF template, or, if you‚Äôre uploading raw HTML, you can specify any custom styles or layout adjustments in your HTML code. Our system will keep your styles intact when generating the PDF.

  4. Step 4: Download Your PDF

    Once the PDF is generated, click 'Generate' and download your PDF. Whether you're using Shopify data or raw HTML, the result will be a ready-to-use PDF document that you can print, email, or store digitally.
